Tree Coin Tokenomics

What is TON Blockchain?

Tree Coin is built on The Open Network (TON), a fast, secure, and scalable blockchain platform originally designed by Telegram. TON offers high transaction throughput and low fees, making it an ideal choice for Tree Coin's mission of environmental sustainability. Its energy-efficient consensus mechanism aligns perfectly with our values.
